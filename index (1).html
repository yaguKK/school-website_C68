ABOUT

Know about Kendriya Vidyalaya ISLAND GROUNDS, Chennai: Board & Curriculum, Admission Procedure, Fees & Fee Structure, Contact Details, Facilities and Ranking, Rating and Reviews. Kendriya Vidyalaya ISLAND GROUNDS Chennai, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day School, with classes from I to XII. It is an Hindi, English Medium school. The school is located in Park Town area of Chennai. Kendriya Vidyalaya ISLAND GROUNDS was established in 1981. It is a Government School and is part of Kendriya Vidyalaya Sangathan (KVS) and is managed by Defence.
